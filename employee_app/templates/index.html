Employee Management System

This is a Django-based web application designed to simplify employee management. It allows users to perform essential operations such as adding, updating, removing, and viewing employee details. The system provides a user-friendly interface for managing employee records, ensuring efficient organization and quick access to information. It is suitable for small to medium-sized businesses looking to streamline their employee management processes.
